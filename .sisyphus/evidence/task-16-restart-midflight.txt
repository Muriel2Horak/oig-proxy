Evidence Mapping To Expected Results
- Restart mid-command is modeled as session change (proxy restart invalidates in-flight ownership).
- Deterministic safe error: operations against an inflight tx from the old session are rejected (INV-2) rather than incorrectly completing.

Key Assertions Covered By Tests
- Session change invalidates old transaction operations:
  - tests/test_twin_e2e_roundtrip.py::TestCrossSessionFailure::test_session_change_invalidates_old_transaction

  - tests/test_digital_twin.py::TestINV2SessionTransaction::test_ack_from_old_session_rejected
- finish_inflight against old-session inflight raises INV-2 (prevents silent clearing/incorrect completion):
  - tests/test_digital_twin.py::TestINV2SessionTransaction::test_finish_inflight_validates_session
