Key Findings
------------
1. Both deliveries are cross-session failures: BOX closes connection normally ~24s after
   receiving Setting frame, then reconnects, but ACK never arrives.
2. ACK timeout fires 34-35s after delivery (configured effective_timeout=30s, with ~4s check interval).
3. Connection closes with Reason=normal - BOX does not report error.
4. BOX reconnects within 6-10s of close, but new connection does NOT carry ACK(Reason=Setting).
5. Connection carrying delivery has only 1 frame (Case 1) or 11 frames (Case 2) - the Setting
   frame is injected into regular poll cycle.
6. Subsequent connections after failure continue normal data polling without retries.
